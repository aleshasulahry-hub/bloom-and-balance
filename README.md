# bloom-and-balance
Bloom & Balance is a women’s wellness and self-growth web application designed to support users in building healthy, sustainable daily routines within a nurturing and uplifting digital space.

The purpose of this platform is to bring together essential self-care and wellness tools into one centralized, easy-to-use website. Rather than relying on multiple disconnected apps, users can stay organized, reflect on their emotions, and care for their physical and mental well-being all in one place. Bloom & Balance is rooted in the belief that true wellness goes beyond productivity - it is about balance, rest, reflection, and consistency.

The application offers a wide range of features tailored to support everyday wellness. These include habit tracking, journaling, mood logging, water intake tracking, meal planning, and reminders. Additionally, it provides a comprehensive cycle and period tracker, complete with educational insights into each phase, personalized wellness tips, cycle-based workout suggestions, and nourishing meal ideas.

Bloom & Balance also extends its support to mothers and families by including tools such as grocery list organization, meal inspiration, and a simple routine planner for managing both personal and family responsibilities.

At its core, the goal of Bloom & Balance is to empower users to grow through small, intentional daily actions - helping them create a more balanced, mindful, and fulfilling lifestyle.
