        🌙 Cycle Phases

        Menstrual

        Lower energy. Focus on rest, iron-rich foods, hydration, and gentle movement.

        Follicular

        Energy rises. Great for fresh habits, productivity, lighter meals, and workouts.

        Ovulation

        Often the most energetic and social phase. Good for higher intensity movement and protein.

        Luteal

        Slow down a little. Support cravings with nourishing foods and calming routines.

        🌿 Cycle-Based Workout Suggestions

        Menstrual

        Walks, stretching, mobility, light yoga

        Follicular

        Pilates, brisk walks, beginner strength

        Ovulation

        Dance, cardio, strength circuits

        Luteal

        Walking, moderate workouts, stretching
